Our Services

At Chenjeez Security Firm, we offer a variety of services tailored to ensure the safety of your personnel, assets, and events. Our services are designed to meet your specific needs and exceed your expectations.

Close Protection

Our trained security personnel will provide immediate and ongoing protection for high-profile individuals, ensuring their safety and well-being in all situations, both personal and professional.

Event Security

We offer comprehensive security services for corporate events, product launches, shareholder meetings, and other large gatherings, ensuring a safe environment for all attendees.

Risk Assessments

Our team will conduct a thorough risk assessment of your facilities, assets, and personnel to identify potential security threats and develop strategies to mitigate risks.

Secure Transportation

Our secure transportation service ensures the safe transit of your personnel, offering peace of mind during travel and safeguarding against any potential threats on the road.

Consultation Services

We offer consultation services to help clients assess their specific security needs, providing expert advice on how to create and implement comprehensive security plans.

Security Equipment

We supply the latest security equipment, including surveillance systems, communication devices, and defensive tools, to ensure maximum protection for our clients.
